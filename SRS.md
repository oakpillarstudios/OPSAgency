**Enterprise Full Stack Digital Agency Platform**

**Software Requirements Specification (SRS)**

**PART 1 — PROJECT OVERVIEW, VISION & BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS**

**1\. Project Overview**

Develop a **premium enterprise-grade full stack digital agency platform** for **OakPillar Studios**.

The platform should not resemble a traditional agency website. Instead, it should combine the premium aesthetics of Apple, Stripe, Vercel, Framer, Linear, and Webflow with the service discovery experience of Fiverr.

Unlike Fiverr, the platform must not allow third-party sellers or freelancers. Every service available on the platform belongs exclusively to OakPillar Studios.

The website will function as:

-   Premium Agency Website
-   Service Marketplace
-   Lead Generation Platform
-   CRM Platform
-   Portfolio Showcase
-   Website Template Marketplace
-   SEO Content Platform
-   Business Automation Showcase
-   Client Acquisition System

The entire project must be developed with scalability in mind so that additional modules such as payments, subscriptions, client dashboards, project management, AI tools, and team collaboration can be integrated without restructuring the existing architecture.

**2\. Business Objectives**

The primary objectives are:
